About StudyTyper

StudyTyper is a lightweight typing practice application focused on making the process of re-typing study notes engaging. By repeatedly typing your own material, you not only memorise it better but also improve your typing speed and accuracy.

This project draws inspiration from typing games such as TypeRacer, yet it keeps the interface minimal and privacy-friendly—your data never leaves your browser.
