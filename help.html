How to Use StudyTyper

  1. Prepare your text: Copy any study notes, article, or sample text you want to practise.
  2. Paste or upload: On the Home page, paste the text into the large textbox or use Upload .txt to load a local file.
  3. Start the session: Click Start Practice. A 3-second countdown will appear before typing begins.
  4. Type: Follow the highlighted characters. Your WPM, accuracy, and other stats update live.
  5. Review results: When you finish, a summary modal appears. Your session is saved locally and shown in the History table.
  6. Settings & History: Use the ⚙️ Settings button on the Home page to adjust preferences or export/import your history.
Need more help? File an issue on the project repository.
